Rules

In the Kitty Jungle, the player encounters new feral cats, and befriends them with careful pets.

Click the "pet" button to pet the cat.

At any time, you can click the arrow button to move on to the next level.

To win the level and get a special badge, you must pet the cat enough times to befriend it. When you have made a new cat friend, you will be prompted to go to the next level to meet more cats.

But be careful...if you choose not to move on to the next level, and keep petting your cat friend, they might not like it...

You lose the game if a cat attacks you!

About

Kitty Jungle is a real place!

Inspired by the backyard of my Bed-Stuy apartment in the summer of 2015, when I used to spend whole afternoons watching the feral colony below.

Because my backyard was wildly overgrown with weeds, my friends and I dubbed it "kitty jungle," and narrated daily episodes of drama and kitten-y cuteness.
